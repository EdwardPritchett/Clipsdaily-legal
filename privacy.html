Privacy Policy — Clips Daily

Last updated: May 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Clips Daily ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you connect your social media accounts (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, X) to our Service, we receive:

  • Account identifier (username/handle)
  • OAuth access tokens (encrypted and stored locally)
  • Basic profile information (display name, profile image URL)

Usage Information

  • Clips you approve or reject through our Discord-based review interface
  • Timestamps of content publication
  • Performance metrics from connected platforms (views, engagement) where made available

Content Information

  • Video clips identified by our system
  • Generated titles, descriptions, and metadata for approved clips
  • Caption text generated from audio transcription

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate your connected accounts
  • Publish approved clips to your designated accounts
  • Provide analytics about your published content
  • Improve our content recommendation systems
  • Maintain Service security and reliability

4. Information Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your account credentials with anyone
  • Publish content without your explicit approval
  • Use your information for advertising purposes

We DO share information only when:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to publish your approved content to platforms you have authorized
  • Necessary to protect rights, property, or safety

5. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens are stored encrypted on our local servers
  • Database access is restricted to authenticated systems only
  • Tokens can be revoked at any time by you through the connected platform's settings
  • No data is transmitted to external services beyond what is necessary to publish approved content

6. Third-Party Services

Our Service connects to third-party platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, X). Use of these platforms is governed by their own privacy policies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Revoke account access at any time through the connected platform
  • Discontinue use of the Service at any time

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide the Service. Upon account disconnection:

  • OAuth tokens are deleted within 30 days
  • Personal account information is removed from active systems
  • Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for service improvement

9. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected information from a minor, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Users

If you access our Service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States, where our infrastructure is located.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to active users. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
